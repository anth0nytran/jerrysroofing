## Key Observations
1. **The site is Divi-heavy** — lots of visual builder markup, slow rendering
2. **Neon lime accent (#E3FE08) is bold and distinctive** — strong brand differentiator but may need refinement for accessibility
3. **Navy + neon lime is a unique combo** for roofing — stands out from typical red/blue/gray palettes
4. **Content is education-first** — storm info, maintenance tips, insurance guidance
5. **Strong local positioning** — "local business, low overhead, community invested"
6. **Missing elements:** No visible testimonials/reviews on homepage, no team photos, no certifications badges, no Google review integration
7. **Two different emails appear:** jerrysroofinginfo@gmail.com (header) vs jerryroofinginfo@gmail.com (footer) — possible typo
