01

Executive Summary

The Katy, TX roofing market is dominated by companies that combine heavy social proof (500+ Google reviews), manufacturer certifications (GAF Master Elite, Owens Corning Platinum), and modern, conversion-optimized websites with interactive estimation tools.

Jerry's Roofing has genuine competitive advantages — a distinctive brand palette, authentic local positioning, and uniquely valuable storm damage content — but currently scores 37/80 against top competitors averaging 67/80. The primary gaps are in social proof visibility, digital tooling, and content depth.

The good news: these gaps are fixable. With a premium website rebuild informed by what the top 10% actually do, Jerry's Roofing can leapfrog the mid-tier competitors and compete directly with the market leaders.

Jerry's Current SEO Gaps

No location-specific landing pages. Single-page site with minimal crawlable content. No blog or resource section. No schema markup for LocalBusiness. Missing meta descriptions and proper heading hierarchy due to Divi builder limitations.

Quick Win Opportunities

City landing pages — Create dedicated pages for Katy, Fulshear, Cypress, Brookshire, Sugar Land, Sealy, and West Houston. Driven Roofing has 15+ of these and ranks for each city name.

Storm content hub — Jerry's existing disaster table is unique content no competitor has. Expand it into a full resource section targeting "storm damage roof repair Houston" and "insurance claim deadline Texas."

Service pages — Individual pages for roof repair, replacement, maintenance, inspections, gutters, and exterior work. Each competitor has these; Jerry's has them all on one page.

05

Patterns of the Top 10%

1

Trust Signals Above the Fold

Every top site puts review counts, star ratings, certifications, and years of experience in the hero section. Amstill leads with "1,200+ 4.9-Star Reviews" immediately below their headline. Jerry's has zero social proof above the fold.

2

Interactive Estimation Tools

The best sites (Onit with Roofr, 314 with cost calculator, Mighty Dog with Roofle) give visitors an immediate, interactive way to get a ballpark price. This captures leads before they bounce. Jerry's has no interactive tools.

3

Manufacturer Certifications as Trust Anchors

Top competitors hold 3-4 certifications at high tiers (GAF Master Elite, Owens Corning Platinum, CertainTeed Master Shingle Applicator). Jerry's shows manufacturer logos but doesn't display certification levels or badges.

4

Location-Specific Landing Pages

Driven Roofing has 15+ city pages. Onit has location-targeted content. This is the #1 local SEO play. Jerry's mentions service areas in a paragraph — no dedicated pages targeting each city.

5

Financing + Warranty as Conversion Levers

3 of 5 top competitors prominently display financing options and extended workmanship warranties (Onit: 10-year). These reduce buyer hesitation at the decision point. Jerry's mentions neither.

06

Recommended Design Direction

Color Palette

Keep Jerry's distinctive navy + lime identity — it's the strongest brand differentiator in a market drowning in blue/red/white. Refine for accessibility:

Navy #0A2E5B Lime #C8D900 Dark #1A1A1A Light #F8F8F5

Shift the neon lime (#E3FE08) slightly darker to #C8D900 for WCAG AA compliance on white backgrounds while keeping the energy.

Typography

Headings: Keep a condensed, bold typeface (similar to Teko) for the punchy, no-nonsense personality. Consider upgrading to Barlow Condensed for a more refined version of the same energy.

Body: Open Sans is solid but generic. Upgrade to DM Sans or Inter for a more modern, geometric feel that pairs better with condensed headings.

Layout & Animation

Dark navy hero section with lime accents and scroll-triggered animations. Parallax roofing imagery. Before/after slider with smooth transitions. Cinematic section transitions inspired by Apple/Stripe. 3D scroll-triggered hero placeholder for premium visual impact.

What to Avoid

Generic blue (every competitor uses it). Template-looking Divi layouts. Stock photography without context. Cluttered trust badge walls. Aggressive pop-up lead capture.

The Strategic Opportunity

Jerry's Roofing can own the "honest local roofer" position that no competitor fully claims. The combination of:

  • Distinctive brand identity (navy + lime, no one else has this)
  • Authentic community story (Jerry Pilley's personal investment in Katy)
  • Education-first content (storm deadlines, maintenance guidance, insurance help)
  • Premium website execution (proving that a local roofer can look better than the corporates)

This positions Jerry's as the anti-corporate choice — the roofer who knows your neighborhood, charges less because of low overhead, and proves expertise through education rather than aggressive sales.
